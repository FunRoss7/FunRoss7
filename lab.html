01
McCall Job Search
Decision model · interactive
A model of a simple question: when should you accept a job offer?
You’ll get a sequence of offers. Accept one and it’s yours. Reject it and wait for the next. It sounds straightforward until you’re in it.
The model underneath has a clean answer. The experience of getting there usually doesn’t.
